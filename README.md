# Learning Biology

Molecular biology, genetics, evolution, neuroscience, and computational biology.

## Books

### Molecular Biology and Genetics
- [Molecular Biology of the Cell](https://www.amazon.com/Molecular-Biology-Cell-Bruce-Alberts/dp/0393884821) - Alberts et al. The standard reference. Encyclopedic but readable.
- [Molecular Biology of the Gene](https://www.amazon.com/Molecular-Biology-Gene-James-Watson/dp/0321762436) - Watson et al. From the co-discoverer of DNA's structure.
- [The Selfish Gene](https://www.amazon.com/Selfish-Gene-Anniversary-Landmark-Science/dp/0198788606) - Richard Dawkins. Gene-centered view of evolution. Influential and accessible.
- [Gene Machine](https://www.amazon.com/Gene-Machine-Quest-Decipher-Secrets/dp/0465093167) - Venki Ramakrishnan. The race to solve the ribosome structure, by a Nobel laureate.

### Evolution
- [The Origin of Species](https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/1228) - Charles Darwin. Free online. Still worth reading the original.
- [The Extended Phenotype](https://www.amazon.com/Extended-Phenotype-Reach-Gene-Landmark/dp/0198788916) - Richard Dawkins. Genes influence the world beyond the organism.
- [Wonderful Life](https://www.amazon.com/Wonderful-Life-Burgess-Nature-History/dp/039330700X) - Stephen Jay Gould. Contingency in evolution via the Burgess Shale.
- [The Blind Watchmaker](https://www.amazon.com/Blind-Watchmaker-Evidence-Evolution-Universe/dp/0393351491) - Richard Dawkins. Natural selection as a designing force.
- [Arrival of the Fittest](https://www.amazon.com/Arrival-Fittest-Nature-Innovates/dp/1617230219) - Andreas Wagner. Where biological innovation comes from.

### Biochemistry and Biophysics
- [Lehninger Principles of Biochemistry](https://www.amazon.com/Lehninger-Principles-Biochemistry-David-Nelson/dp/1319228003) - Nelson & Cox. The standard biochemistry textbook.
- [Physical Biology of the Cell](https://www.amazon.com/Physical-Biology-Cell-Rob-Phillips/dp/0815344503) - Phillips et al. Physics meets biology. Quantitative and modern.
- [Random Walks in Biology](https://www.amazon.com/Random-Walks-Biology-Howard-Berg/dp/0691000646) - Howard Berg. Short, elegant intro to stochastic processes in biology.

### Neuroscience
- [Principles of Neural Science](https://www.amazon.com/Principles-Neural-Science-Sixth-Kandel/dp/1259642232) - Kandel et al. The definitive neuroscience textbook.
- [The Brain That Changes Itself](https://www.amazon.com/Brain-That-Changes-Itself-Frontiers/dp/0143113100) - Norman Doidge. Neuroplasticity for a general audience.
- [Behave](https://www.amazon.com/Behave-Biology-Humans-Best-Worst/dp/0143110918) - Robert Sapolsky. Biology of human behavior from neurons to society.
- [Theoretical Neuroscience](https://www.amazon.com/Theoretical-Neuroscience-Computational-Mathematical-Modeling/dp/0262541858) - Dayan & Abbott. Computational neuroscience textbook.

### Systems Biology
- [An Introduction to Systems Biology](https://www.amazon.com/Introduction-Systems-Biology-Mathematical-Computational/dp/1439837172) - Uri Alon. Design principles of biological circuits. Elegant.
- [Systems Biology: A Textbook](https://www.amazon.com/Systems-Biology-Textbook-Edda-Klipp/dp/352733558X) - Klipp et al. Comprehensive.
- [Biological Modelling and Simulation](https://www.amazon.com/Biological-Modeling-Simulation-Quantitative-Computational/dp/0262195844) - Schwartz. Computational approaches.

### Computational Biology and Bioinformatics
- [Biological Sequence Analysis](https://www.amazon.com/Biological-Sequence-Analysis-Probabilistic-Proteins/dp/0521629713) - Durbin et al. HMMs, sequence alignment, phylogenetics.
- [An Introduction to Bioinformatics Algorithms](https://www.amazon.com/Introduction-Bioinformatics-Algorithms-Computational-Molecular/dp/0262101068) - Jones & Pevzner. Algorithms-first approach.
- [Deep Learning for the Life Sciences](https://www.amazon.com/Deep-Learning-Life-Sciences-Microscopy/dp/1492039837) - Bharath Ramsundar et al. ML applied to biology.

### General / History
- [The Double Helix](https://www.amazon.com/Double-Helix-Personal-Discovery-Structure/dp/074321630X) - James Watson. The discovery of DNA's structure. Candid and controversial.
- [What Is Life?](https://www.amazon.com/What-Life-Physical-Aspect-Living/dp/1107604664) - Erwin Schr√∂dinger. A physicist's view of biology. Influenced the discovery of DNA.
- [The Eighth Day of Creation](https://www.amazon.com/Eighth-Day-Creation-Revolution-Commemorative/dp/0879694785) - Horace Freeland Judson. The history of molecular biology. Masterful.
- [I Contain Multitudes](https://www.amazon.com/Contain-Multitudes-Microbes-Within-Grander/dp/0062368591) - Ed Yong. The microbiome and symbiosis.
- [The Vital Question](https://www.amazon.com/Vital-Question-Evolution-Origins-Complex/dp/0393352978) - Nick Lane. Energy and the origin of complex life.
- [The Gene: An Intimate History](https://www.amazon.com/Gene-Intimate-History-Siddhartha-Mukherjee/dp/1432837818) - Siddhartha Mukherjee.

## Papers

### Foundational
- [Molecular Structure of Nucleic Acids](https://www.nature.com/articles/171737a0) - Watson & Crick (1953). The DNA structure paper. One page.
- [On Protein Synthesis](https://profiles.nlm.nih.gov/spotlight/sc/catalog/nlm:nlmuid-101584582X366-doc) - Crick (1958). The central dogma.
- [A Structure for Deoxyribose Nucleic Acid](https://www.nature.com/articles/171737a0) - Watson & Crick (1953).

### Modern / Computational
- [Highly Accurate Protein Structure Prediction with AlphaFold](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-021-03819-2) - Jumper et al. (2021). AlphaFold 2.
- [De novo design of protein structure and function with RFdiffusion](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-023-06415-8) - Watson et al. (2023). Protein design with diffusion models.
- [Scaling Euclidean Transformers for Generative Molecular Modeling](https://arxiv.org/abs/2304.10350) - Molecular generation at scale.
- [A Complete Reference Genome Improves Analysis of Human Genetic Variation](https://www.science.org/doi/10.1126/science.abl3533) - Nurk et al. (2022). T2T complete human genome.

### Evolution and Population Genetics
- [The Neutral Theory of Molecular Evolution](https://www.nature.com/articles/217624a0) - Kimura (1968). Not everything is adaptive.
- [The Spandrels of San Marco](https://faculty.washington.edu/ly} /SpijkerboerWebsite/Readings/GouldLewontin1979.pdf) - Gould & Lewontin (1979). Against adaptationism.

## Courses

## Tools and Databases

- [UniProt](https://www.uniprot.org/) - Protein sequence and function database.
- [NCBI](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/) - National Center for Biotechnology Information.
- [PDB](https://www.rcsb.org/) - Protein Data Bank. 3D structures.
- [AlphaFold Protein Structure Database](https://alphafold.ebi.ac.uk/) - Predicted structures for the proteome.
- [Biopython](https://biopython.org/) - Python tools for computational biology.
- [Rosalind](https://rosalind.info/) - Bioinformatics coding challenges.
